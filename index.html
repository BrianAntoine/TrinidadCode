Our Mission

TrinidadCode will be a resource to help grow a new generation in Trinidad and Tobago that would be able to work with technology and help bring their knowledge back into their community. This resource would allow many children to learn from their technology that they have at home. With the integrated login system, it allows for the user to track what demonstrations that have completed and what else they may need to work on.

TrinidadCode looks to educate the entire country about coding and how they will be able to use it for themselves TrinidadCode hopes to first reach uts inner community of Sangre Grande and then be able to spread to Arima, and all over the country. The future is bright and the oppourtunites are endless; we are happy to be here for Trinidad!
