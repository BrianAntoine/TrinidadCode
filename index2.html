About Us

Technology is the Future

Coding is a skill that should be taught all throughout the world. Everyday new technology is being created, with new expectations for everyone to adjust and implement technology into their daily lives. However, many third world countries do not have the resources to put value into growing their youth with computing skills. TrinidadCode is a website that supplies children in Trinidad with the resources to learn how to code. TrinidadCode will utilize login systems, Student profile authentication, and calendar updates, as well as a learning curriculum with coding projects and demonstrations designed for students.
